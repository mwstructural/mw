Our Projects

Although MW Consulting Engineers is a newly established firm, our team members bring a wealth of experience from prior roles in leading engineering firms. Below are examples of projects they were involved in before joining MW.

Residential Projects

126 Frank St Development, Gold Coast

Full structural design of 4-storey residential concrete building.

Post-tensioned transfer beams and reinforced concrete slabs for efficient floor layouts.

Coordination with architectural and services teams for seamless integration.

St Vicent Provate Hospital Norht Theater Building, Brisbane

Full structural design of 7-storey reinforced concrete healthcare facility.

Design of 1 m thick reinforced concrete radiation bunker walls to meet shielding standards.

Coordination with hospital services for uninterrupted operations during construction.

Industrial Projects

Willoughby Leisure Centre, Sydney

Structural design of long-span timber portal frames (26 m and 34 m clear spans).

Connection detailing and design for prefabricated timber elements.

Coordination with architectural team to maintain exposed timber aesthetics.

BMX Stadium Cover, Palmerston, NT

Structural design of a 68 m clear-span steel roof structure, engineered for high cyclonic wind loads in accordance with AS/NZS 1170.2 and Region D requirements.

Design review of portal frames, rafters, purlins, and bracing systems for structural stability and durability over the 110 m length.

Design for erection methodology and transportation limitations.

Commercial Projects

Tarneit Shopping Centre, Melbourne

Structural design of large-span retail buildings including Coles, Kmart, gym, and specialty tenancies.

Design of structural steel roof systems, concrete ground slabs, and suspended bondek slabs for gym areas, bored pile footings for high axial loads from wall panels and columns.

Precast concrete wall panel design, including panel lifting, bracing, and connection details.
