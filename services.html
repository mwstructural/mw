Our Services

Residential Structural Engineering

We specialize in structural engineering for residential buildings, including new homes, renovations, extensions, and multi-unit developments.

  • Structural design for new houses and townhouses
  • Renovation and extension structural assessments and designs
  • Foundation and footing design
  • Retaining walls and basement structural solutions
  • Defect inspections and structural certification

Industrial Structural Engineering

We design robust structural solutions for warehouses, factories, and manufacturing plants.

  • Steel frame design and detailing
  • Industrial floor slabs and load-bearing assessments
  • Pre-engineered building design support
  • Structural audits and defect rectification
  • Site inspections and project consultancy

Commercial Structural Engineering

We deliver tailored structural engineering services for retail, office, hospitality, and mixed-use commercial developments.

  • Structural design for shopping centres, supermarkets, and gyms
  • Multi-storey commercial building framing and slab systems
  • Precast panel design and connection detailing
  • Car park structures and suspended slabs
  • Coordination with architectural and services teams for integrated solutions
