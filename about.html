About MW Consulting Engineers

MW Consulting Engineers is a Brisbane-based consultancy offering practical, buildable, and cost-effective structural solutions. We work across a wide range of projects including residential buildings, industrial structures, and commercial developments.

Our approach focuses on clarity, constructability, and compliance. We collaborate closely with architects, builders, and owners to ensure structural designs are efficient and tailored to real-world conditions.

Our Services

We provide structural design, analysis, inspections, and documentation for:

  • New builds, extensions, and renovations
  • Steel, concrete, timber, and masonry structures
  • Industrial warehouses and framing systems
  • Footing designs, retaining walls, and floor slabs
  • Defect reports and site inspections

Our Commitment

MW Consulting Engineers is committed to delivering timely and accurate engineering outcomes. All work complies with current Australian Standards and local council requirements. We aim to build long-term relationships based on trust, reliability, and technical excellence.
